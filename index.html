About Me

I am a Lecturer in Department of Computing and Emerging Technologies at The Millennium Universal College (TMUC), Islamabad - a Transnational Education (TNE) institution delivering the BSc Computing program in association with the University of Hertfordshire. I am also an active AI/ML researcher with nine peer-reviewed publications across IEEE, Springer, and MDPI venues.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science (Data Science) from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), Vietnam National University, where my supervisor-evaluated thesis under A/Prof. Dr. Pham Hoang Anh developed an optimized RBF-SVM classifier achieving 95.24% accuracy for ophthalmic disease detection. The work was published at Springer GOODTECHS 2024 (LNICST, Scopus indexed).

My ongoing research includes TabViT a Tabular Vision Transformer for heart disease classification (targeting MDPI MAKE), a systematic review on ML/DL for multimodal neurodegenerative disease diagnosis, and an XAI survey paper for a Q1 journal. My research collaborations span Kuwait (Dr. Jibran Khan Yousafzai, AUK), Malaysia (Abdulraqeb Alhammadi, UTM), Vietnam (Dr. Pham Hoang Anh, HCMUT), and Australia (Deakin University).

I am actively applying for full-time PhD positions and Research Assistant roles (Europe, America, and beyond). If you are interested in collaboration or supervision, please reach out via email or LinkedIn.

Master of Engineering in Computer Science (Data Science)

Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT) · VNU-HCM, Vietnam

Thesis: Machine Learning Approaches for Detecting Ophthalmic Diseases.

Supervisor: A/Prof. Dr. Pham Hoang Anh.

Achieved 95.24% accuracy in cataract detection using an optimized RBF-SVM classifier, outperforming Random Forest (94.44%) and Polynomial SVM (92.86%) via GridSearchCV tuning. Built a full feature extraction pipeline using HSV color features, Canny edge detection, and Hu moments across a dataset of 658 retinal images. Deep learning models (ResNet50) were benchmarked against traditional ML for fundus image classification. Published at Springer GOODTECHS 2024 (LNICST vol. 649, Scopus indexed).
